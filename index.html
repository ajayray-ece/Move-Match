How to Play

1. Select a difficulty level (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5)

2. Click on any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it

3. Arrange the numbers in ascending order from left to right, top to bottom

4. The empty space should be at the end

5. Try to solve it in minimum moves and time!
